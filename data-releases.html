Kepler and K2 Project Deliveries

There are two archives for official Kepler and K2 data products - the Exoplanet Archive which is hosted at the NASA Exoplanet Science Institute (NExScI) and the Mikulski Archive for Space Telescopes (MAST) which is located at the Space Telescope Science Institute (STScI). The Exoplanet Archive primarily hosts data related to the Kepler mission planet search while the MAST is responsible for hosting time series data and spacecraft calibration products for Kepler and K2.

Kepler Products

Kepler Long and Short Cadence Target Pixel Files Pixel image data for all targets observed by Kepler during Quarter 0 to Quarter 17 MAST Kepler Archive Manual
Kepler Long and Short Cadence Light Curves Time series data for targets observed by Kepler during Quarter 0 to Quarter 17 for which a light curve was created MAST Kepler Archive Manual
Kepler Full Frame Images Full MAST Kepler Archive Manual
Commissioning Data Commissioning Various Various
Kepler Cotrending Basis Vectors Files Q0-Q17 MAST Kepler Archive Manual

K2 Products

K2 Long and Short Cadence Target Pixel Files Pixel image data for all targets observed by K2 during Campaign 0 onward MAST Kepler Archive Manual
Two-wheel Concept Engineering Test Target Pixel Files Pixel image data for all targets observed during a 9-day two-wheel engineering test MAST GO Office Blog Post
K2 Long and Short Cadence Light Curves Time series data for targets observed by K2 during Campaign 3 onward for which a light curve was created MAST Kepler Archive Manual
K2 Full Frame Images Full images of all 84 channels. Typically, two are taken per campaign. MAST Kepler Archive Manual
Ecliptic Plane Input Catalog Full images of all 84 channels. Typically, two are taken per campaign. MAST Kepler Archive Manual
Thruster Firing Information A list of cadences when the thrusters fired to reset pointing for Campaign 1 and Campaign 2 MAST Thruster Firing Readme File
