E-mail

You can contact the Guest Observer Office at keplergo@mail.arc.nasa.gov for advice and information on:

  • the Kepler or K2 missions;
  • instrument characteristics;
  • data and calibration issues;
  • data analysis software.

Your contact information will be used to answer your query and will not be used for commercial purposes or given away except as required by law. For more information, consult the NASA Privacy Policy.

Twitter

You can also tweet your questions to @KeplerGO, or search for hashtag #k2help on Twitter to look at questions asked by others.

FAQ

The FAQ archives at MAST for Kepler and K2 answer many questions about the missions as well as how to retrieve and analyze the data. The FAQ archives on the original Kepler/K2 website can be accessed here for Kepler and here for K2.
