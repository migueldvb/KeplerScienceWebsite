Publication database

Summary

The Guest Observer office curates a list of scientific publications pertaining to Kepler and K2. The database contains 1546 publications, of which 1360 are peer-reviewed. It demonstrates the important impact of Kepler/K2 data on astronomical research.

You can access the publication list by mission:

  • Kepler publications »
  • K2 publications »

Or by topic:

  • Exoplanet publications »
  • Astrophysics publications »

If you spot an error in the database, such as a missing entry, please get in touch or open an issue in the GitHub repository of the database.

Last update: 27 Oct 2015.

Breakdown by subject

Both Kepler and K2 data have been used for scientific applications that reach far beyond exoplanet research. While 750 works relate to exoplanets (49%), a total of 796 pertain to other areas of astrophysics (51%).
