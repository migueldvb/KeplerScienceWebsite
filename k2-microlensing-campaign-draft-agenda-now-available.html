K2 Microlensing Campaign Draft Agenda Now Available

Date Fri 24 April 2015 By Thomas Barclay

A draft agenda is now available for the K2 Microlensing Workshop. This will likely be updated as we get closer to the workshop.

The venue for the workshop will be the SETI Institute in Mountain View, CA. Note that this is a change to the originally advertised venue.

There will be talks from Steve Howell (K2 Project Scientist, NASA Ames), Tom Barclay (K2 Guest Observer Office Director, NASA Ames), Doug Hudgins (NASA Exoplanet Exploration Program Scientist and K2 Deputy Program Scientist, NASA HQ) and Fergal Mullally (K2 Science Officer, NASA Ames), in addition to contributed talks from members of the scientific community.
